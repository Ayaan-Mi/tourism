Rock Garden

  • The Rock Garden Chandigarh is a 35 acre eco-friendly garden, built solely by home waste and other Industrial items.

  • Sculptures are created using items such as bangles, ceramic pots, tiles, bottles and Electric waste.

  • Garden is a mixture of Landscape, architecture, sculpture and mythology.

  • NEK CHAND, founder of Rock Garden, started creating this garden secretly during his spare time in 1957.

  • The Layout of Garden is based on the fantasy of a Lost Kingdom. It has 14 different chambers like the forecourt.

  • The Teej festival, when the Rock Garden assumes a festive look, holds a special attraction for tourists

Things to Do at the Rock Garden, Chandigarh

  • 1. Explore the Sculptures: The Rock Garden's magic comes alive through its enchanting sculptures. Wander through various sections, each telling its own story with unique themes and artistic expressions. Be mesmerized by the craftsmanship that turns discarded items like broken bangles, tiles, and glass into breathtaking art.

  • 2. Marvel at the Man-Made Waterfalls: Take a moment to enjoy the beauty of cascading waterfalls, gentle streams, and tranquil pools scattered throughout the garden. The soothing sounds of flowing water and the stunning visuals create a serene atmosphere. Snap some photos, but tread carefully to avoid a surprise splash!

  • 3. Discover the Aquariums: Hidden among the rock formations are several aquariums that capture the imagination of visitors of all ages. Admire the vibrant fish in various shapes, sizes, and colors. Look out for fascinating sea life like sharks, starfish, and stingrays.

  • 4. Immerse Yourself in Nature: Amidst the artistic installations, the Rock Garden embraces nature's beauty. Take a leisurely stroll through lush greenery, admire blooming flowers, and relish the peaceful surroundings. It's a perfect spot to reconnect with nature and relax.

  • 5. Plan a Picnic: The Rock Garden offers designated picnic spots where you can unwind with friends and family. Enjoy snacks amid the picturesque settings, surrounded by unique sculptures. For convenience, grab quick bites from the food stalls and eateries at the end of your garden tour.

  • 6. Experience Cultural Events at the Amphitheatre: Don't miss the chance to witness vibrant cultural performances at the amphitheatre. From music and dance to theatrical shows, this space hosts a variety of events. Check the schedule to catch a performance and experience the Rock Garden's cultural flair.

  • 7. Indulge in Photography: The Rock Garden is a paradise for photographers. Capture the intricate details of the sculptures, the play of light and shadow, and the overall whimsical ambiance. Each corner offers a unique perspective, providing endless opportunities for stunning shots.

  • 8. Challenge Yourself in the Maze Garden: Test your sense of direction in the maze garden. Navigate through twists and turns, experiencing the thrill of finding your way. This fun activity appeals to visitors of all ages and adds an adventurous element to your visit.

  • 9. Shop for Unique Souvenirs: Before you leave, stop by the souvenir shop to browse through a selection of handicrafts, artifacts, and other items made by local artisans. These unique pieces make for memorable keepsakes or gifts to bring back home.
