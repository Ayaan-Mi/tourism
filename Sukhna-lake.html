Sukhna Lake

  • Sukhna Lake is a 3 sq. km rainfed lake created in 1958 by damming the Sukhna Choe, a seasonal streamcoming down from Shivalik hills.

  • Sukhna Lake is a sanctuary for many exotic migratory birds like- Siberian Duck, Storks and Cranes during winter month.

  • The Lake is declared National Wetland by Government of India.

  • The Sukhna Lake, has longest channel for rowing and yachting events in Asia.

"Explore Lake Activities"

  • 1. Boating :- "Experience the serene beauty of Sukhna Lake with an unforgettable boating adventure! Best of all, there's no entry fee, making it an accessible escape for everyone. For a relaxing ride, take a two-person boat, or gather your friends and family for a four-person boat. Enjoy a luxurious ride in a Cruise, or a circular boat ideal for groups in Donut/Photon, or get a taste of traditional boating in Shikara. Whether you're seeking a peaceful retreat or a fun outing, boating on Sukhna Lake promises a delightful experience that won't break the bank."

    Charges:

    Two-Person: ₹50
    Four-Person: ₹100
    Cruise Boat: ₹325 per person for half an hour.
    Donut/Photon Boat: ₹450 per person for 30 minutes.
    Shikara Boat: ₹450 for two people for 30 minutes.

  • 2. Sunset Watching: Experience the awe-inspiring sunset over Sukhna Lake, where the evening sky unfolds a mesmerizing tapestry of colors. It's a breathtaking moment that fills the air with a sense of wonder.

  • 3. Photography: Sukhna Lake is a paradise for photographers. Capture stunning views of the lake's serene waters, the vibrant reflections, and the diverse birdlife that graces its shores.

  • 4. Morning Walks and Jogging: Start your day with a refreshing walk or jog around the lake. The pathways are ideal for enjoying the peaceful ambiance while staying active, whether in the early morning light or during a tranquil evening stroll.

  • 5. Birdwatching: During winter, Sukhna Lake becomes a haven for migratory birds. It's a delight for birdwatching enthusiasts, offering the chance to spot elegant Siberian ducks, cranes, and storks as they make their seasonal stopover.

  • 6. Picnicking: Gather your loved ones and head to Sukhna Lake for a delightful picnic. With its lush greenery and serene surroundings, it's the perfect setting for a relaxing day outdoors, complete with a basket of goodies.

  • 7. Visit the Garden of Silence: Just a stone's throw from the lake, the Garden of Silence provides a peaceful retreat for meditation and contemplation. It's a beautiful space to find your inner calm and enjoy a moment of quiet reflection.
